Deep Learning is a specialized branch of Machine Learning that uses artificial neural networks to learn complex patterns from large amounts of data.

Deep learning models are inspired by the structure of the human brain and contain multiple layers of interconnected neurons.

Neural networks consist of input layers, hidden layers, and output layers that process information and generate predictions.

Deep Learning is commonly used in image recognition, natural language processing, speech recognition, and autonomous systems.

Popular deep learning frameworks include TensorFlow, PyTorch, and Keras.

Deep learning requires large datasets and significant computational power, but it can achieve highly accurate results in complex tasks.
